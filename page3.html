ABOUT THE GYMMBOXX FAMILY

ESTABLISHED IN OCTOBER 2010, GYMMBOXX STARTED AS A SINGLE GYM SITUATED AT THE TOP OF A MULTI-STOREY CAR PARK IN SINGAPORE (BEDOK RESERVOIR). OVER THE YEARS, GYMMBOXX HAS MADE ITS MARK AS SINGAPORE’S AFFORDABLE PREMIUM GYM BRAND, SERVING THE RESIDENTIAL NEIGHBOURHOODS AND HEARTLANDS. WITH MORE THAN 6 GYMS ISLAND-WIDE, GYMMBOXX WILL CONTINUE TO EXPAND AND BRING QUALITY GYM FACILITIES AND SERVICE TO THE REST OF SINGAPORE AND BEYOND.



                                                                                                                                                               

GYMMBOXX WELCOMES THE PUBLIC TO VISIT OUR GYM WITHOUT HAVING TO PURCHASE A MEMBERSHIP PLAN. WE BELIEVE IN PROVIDING EVERYONE ACCESS TO QUALITY GYM EQUIPMENT, FACILITIES, AND SERVICES WITHOUT IMPOSING A LONG MEMBERSHIP PLAN. OUR PAY-PER-ENTRY ALLOWS THE PUBLIC TO HAVE FULL ACCESS TO ALL GYMMBOXX, WHICH INCLUDES THE 24-HOUR GYMS, ON A VISIT-BY-VISIT BASIS.

                                                                                                                                                               

ALL COMMERCIAL GYMS REQUIRE A MEMBERSHIP CONTRACT THAT REINFORCES THEIR CLIENTS TO STAY AS MEMBERS THROUGH CERTAIN CLAUSES. WE INSTEAD WANT TO GIVE OUR USERS THE FREEDOM TO DECIDE THE BEST VALUE TO SUIT THEIR FITNESS NEEDS. WE PROVIDE LONG AND SHORT TERM MEMBERSHIP PLANS, AND DO NOT OBLIGATE ANY CUSTOMER TO CONTINUE HIS OR HER MEMBERSHIP.

                                                                                                                                                               

GYMMBOXX IS PROUD TO PUBLICISE OUR FIXED RATES FOR OUR MEMBERSHIP PLANS, OUR PER-ENTRY RATES, AND OUR PERSONAL TRAINING RATES. OUR CUSTOMERS CAN EASILY COMPARE OUR INCREDIBLE PRICES WITH OTHER GYM SERVICES, AND NOT HAVE TO WORRY ABOUT PUSHY SALES TACTICS OR HAVING TO NEGOTIATE WITH A GYM MEMBERSHIP CONSULTANT FOR A FAIR RATE. HAVING TRANSPARENT PRICING IS OUR TESTAMENT TO OUR CONFIDENCE IN OUR OUTSTANDING RATES.

                                                                                                                                                               

NEAR THE END OF 2014, THE GYMMBOXX TEAM MADE A BOLD PROPOSAL TO THE BUILDING MANAGEMENT TEAMS THAT HOST OUR GYM – FOR GYMMBOXX TO GO 24 HOURS. ON JAN 1ST 2015, GYMMBOXX BISHAN BEGAN ITS 24-HOUR OPERATION HOURS. SINCE THEN, GYMMBOXX HAS LAUNCHED MORE 24-HOUR GYMS WHENEVER POSSIBLE. WE PROVIDE MIDNIGHT STAFF SO AS TO ENSURE SAFETY AND SERVICE EVEN DURING THE WEE HOURS OF THE DAY. GYMMBOXX REMAINS THE ONLY GYM BRAND PROVIDING 24-HOUR GYM SERVICES THAT WELCOME THE PUBLIC THROUGH PER-ENTRY PAYMENT.

                                                                                                                                                               

GYMMBOXX AIMS TO BE THE NUMBER-ONE, FRIENDLY, NEIGHBOURHOOD, AFFORDABLE PREMIUM GYM BRAND IN SINGAPORE. AS SUCH, WE BELIEVE IN OFFERING PREFERENTIAL RATES FOR MEMBERSHIP PLANS FOR ANY YOUTH (AGES 21 AND BELOW) OR SENIOR CITIZEN (AGES 55 AND ABOVE). THERE IS NO REASON NOT TO WHEN THE GYMMBOXX FAMILY HOPES TO SEE ALL YOUNG PEOPLE AND ELDERLY GET IN SHAPE AND STAY FIT.
